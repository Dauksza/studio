# **App Name**: SkillShowdown

## Core Features:

- Real-time Skill Challenges: Participate in real-time challenges across coding, math, science, and business, with adaptive difficulty scaling to player skill.
- AI Personalized Learning: AI dynamically generates personalized learning paths tailored to individual strengths and weaknesses.
- XP Progression & Leaderboards: Earn experience points (XP) for completing challenges, progressing through levels, and competing on global and friend leaderboards.
- Streak Rewards & Daily Missions: Receive streak rewards for consecutive daily participation and complete daily missions for bonus XP and in-game currency.
- Multiplayer Tournaments: Compete in structured multiplayer tournaments with brackets and prizes, showcasing your skills against other players.
- AI Boss Battles: Engage in boss battles against AI opponents, tool that adjusts strategies dynamically to test mastery of specific skills and concepts.
- Skill Certificates: Earn blockchain-verified skill certificates upon mastering specific skills, providing tangible proof of competence for employers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to represent intelligence and competition.
- Background color: Light gray (#E9ECEF), providing a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Energetic orange (#FF8C00) to highlight key interactive elements and achievements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font providing a modern and neutral feel.
- Use clean, geometric icons that reflect the disciplines featured: coding, math, science, and business.
- Prioritize a clear, intuitive layout that minimizes clutter and guides the user through game challenges, progression, and learning paths.
- Incorporate subtle animations for feedback on user interactions, level transitions, and the successful completion of challenges.
